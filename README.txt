Simple Calculator 

A  Calculator for simple mathematical expression i.e. Addition, Substraction, Division and Multiplication for numbers between 0 and 9;
Written By a Learning Android Developer 

USAGE:

Click on a number and then an operator and finally another number.
Click the "=" sign to view results.
